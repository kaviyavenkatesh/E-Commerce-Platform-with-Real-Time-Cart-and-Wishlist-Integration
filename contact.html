FAQs:

  • Q: What products/services does Dreamy Layers offer?
  • A:

    Dreamy Layers specializes in creating exquisite handcrafted cakes and desserts for all your special occasions. From elegant wedding cakes to whimsical birthday treats, our talented team of bakers and designers are dedicated to bringing your sweetest dreams to life. We use only the finest ingredients to ensure each creation is not only visually stunning but also irresistibly delicious. Whether you're celebrating a wedding, birthday, or any memorable event, let Dreamy Layers be the sweet centerpiece of your joyous moments.

  • Q: How can I place an order?
  • A:

    To place an order with Dreamy Layers, simply follow these easy steps:

    1. Browse our Menu: Explore our delectable menu featuring a variety of cakes, cupcakes, and desserts.
    2. Select Your Favorites: Choose the items you'd like to order and customize them according to your preferences, such as flavors, sizes, and designs.
    3. Add to Cart: Once you've made your selections, add them to your shopping cart for review.
    4. Review Your Order: Double-check your order details, ensuring everything is just the way you want it.
    5. Proceed to Checkout: When you're ready, proceed to the checkout page to provide your delivery details and any special instructions.
    6. Complete Payment: Securely complete your payment using our trusted payment gateway.
    7. Confirmation: After successful payment, you'll receive an order confirmation with all the details. Our team will then start crafting your delightful treats.

    For any assistance or special requests, feel free to contact our friendly customer service team using the information provided above. We look forward to sweetening your celebration!
